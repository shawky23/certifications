UiPath Academy Automation Developer Associate Training

Share PDF Badge

omar Shawky

View All Credentials
The UiPath Academy Automation Developer Associate Training is a collection of curated courses to teach automation beginners how to build components for enterprise-level automation projects driven by seasoned Automation Developers and Solution Architects. The training covers, in depth, the knowledge and skills to develop enterprise automation projects using UiPath Studio, Orchestrator, and Robots. This training includes everything needed to know to pass the UiPath Certified Professional Automation Developer Associate Certification exam. Throughout the training, users learn from a mix of videos, hands-on practices, knowledge checks, and reflection spaces. This curriculum has a final assessment.
